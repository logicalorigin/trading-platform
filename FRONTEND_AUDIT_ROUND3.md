## Cross-cutting themes

1. Green-for-directional-intent is the dominant residual violation: buy/call/bullish/long still renders green (instead of blue) in 15+ spots across the header broadcast stack, FlowScreen spread column, trade strategy chips, algo KPI row labels, MarketDemoScreen hero chips, and six independent helpers inside ResearchChartSurface.tsx — a single semanticToneModel (toneForDirectionalIntent/toneForOptionSide) sweep per file closes all of them.
2. Hand-rolled SegmentedControl clones are the most widespread primitive duplication (~15 sites across Flow, Trade, Backtesting, Research, Diagnostics, Settings, Account, TickerSearch): mapped <button> rows with manual active styling, often missing radiogroup/aria-pressed semantics that the primitive provides for free.
3. Hand-rolled StatTile/MetricChip stat tiles recur in 10+ files (Flow, GEX, Signals, Account, Diagnostics MetricCard, Settings status strip, MobileMoreSheet, PositionRowActionMenu) — StatTile's docstring already names these dialects as consolidation targets.
4. Bare empty/loading states instead of DataUnavailableState/Skeleton appear in 15+ branches (DiagnosticsScreen alone has 6), plus text/emoji spinners in ResearchCalendarView — empty states consistently lack context and recovery affordances.
5. Status indicators (dot+label pills) are hand-rolled instead of StatusPill in Diagnostics, PlatformShell footer, FooterMemoryPressureIndicator, Settings SourceBadge, and AccountScreen transition status.
6. Green misused as a generic selection/active highlight (DiagnosticsScreen tabs+range, SettingsScreen smallButton incl. the dirty Save button) — selection should be the accent token.
7. Not-configured operational states collapse to RED instead of AMBER (PlatformShell FooterStatusField, MobileMoreSheet IBKR chip), and conversely hard failures render AMBER instead of RED in SettingsScreen banners — the 3-state operational scale is being flattened from both ends.
8. Raw hex/rgba/shadow literals bypass tokens (AlgoSaveBar and ChartTickerSearch shadows vs ELEVATION, MachineStateDiagram #e8a13a vs CSS_COLOR.amber, PlatformShell #F7FAFF fallback).
9. Emoji and bare glyphs as decoration/status without the Icon primitive or aria-labels (★ golden markers, 🔔 alert button, ⚡ 0DTE, 📄/📅 tab labels, 🔑/📅 empty-state ornaments, decorative FlaskConical).
10. Clickable divs/rows without keyboard access concentrated in the Research area (PhotonicsObservatory peer/supplier/graph/heatmap cells, ResearchCalendarView earnings rows).
11. Unguarded infinite animations ignore prefers-reduced-motion (PhotonicsObservatory edgeFlow, TradeChainPanel's two inline keyframe spinners) because inline keyframes escape the class-scoped reduced-motion CSS.
