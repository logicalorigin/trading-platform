## Cross-cutting themes

1. **Raw drop-shadow geometry vs `ELEVATION`** — floats/drawers/popovers hand-roll `boxShadow: "0 Npx Mpx <token>"` instead of `ELEVATION.sm/md/lg` (algo column+mobile drawers & slider popover, Flow overlay+column drawer, Account filter drawer, Backtest sticky card, Research theme chips). Token-backed *color* doesn't save the bespoke geometry.
2. **Surface gradients** — two-stop/`linear-gradient` fills for depth where flat `bg` tokens belong (algo `PipelineStrip` stage pills, `OperationsSignalRow` fresh-row fade, Research workspace-card header + ambient page radials, Signals freshness meter).
3. **Decorative backdrop-blur glass** — `backdropFilter: blur()` on modal scrims / sticky headers (algo `CreateDeploymentModal`, Backtest sticky header, Account `PositionProtectionEditor`) vs opaque token surfaces.
4. **Directional/selection green misuse** — green used for selection or directional intent instead of `accent`/`directionBuy`: the **GEX heatmap legend paints call-heavy GREEN while the cells render BLUE** (both highs — it mislabels the chart), Watchlist `Default` selection, Flow news sentiment, Research `meta` chip, plus Signals hand-rolling `toneForDirection`/`toneForTrend` instead of the shared `semanticToneModel`.
5. **Raw type/spacing/color literals vs helpers** — `fontSize:12`/`fontWeight:600` (Market tables, Backtest 54×, algo `headerChipStyle`, Settings), raw `"2px 0"` padding vs `sp()` (Trade panels), and hardcoded `rgba(0,0,0,..)`/hex-alpha vs `cssColorAlpha` (MultiChartGrid rings, NotificationsDrawer scrim, Research chips).
6. **Hand-rolled primitive** — Settings `smallButton()` reimplements the `Button` primitive (25 call sites) that the same file already imports and uses correctly.
