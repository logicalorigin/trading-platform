Proposed v3 · Market page, ground-up

A market command center — your chart grid drops into the center slot

Designed as a dashboard first. Top bar carries the one primary read (regime + key stats). Three panels frame the workspace: a compact Scanner (left, the "what to chart" list), the chart slot (center, dominant — your existing MultiChartGrid drops in here), and Market internals + Context (right — breadth, sector flow, P/C, vol, news, catalysts). Nothing is stacked on the charts; every panel has one job. Blue = bullish, red = bearish, amber = mixed/stale/reference. Sample RISK-ON tape.
